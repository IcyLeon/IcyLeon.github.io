WELCOME TO BOWLING

WHAT IS BOWLING?

Bowling is a sport and recreational activity in which players roll balls toward pins. The goal of pin bowling is to knock over pins on a long playing surface called a lane. Oil is applied in different patterns to the wood or synthetic surface of lanes to provide protective lubrication. In bowling, a strike occurs when all the pins are knocked over on the first roll, and a spare occurs when all the pins are knocked over on the second roll.

PERSONAL EXPERIENCE

JASON BELMONTE

National finals: 30 PBA Tour (14 majors)

Jason Belmonte is an Australian professional ten-pin bowler. He plays on the PBA Tour in the United States and in world events. He is known for being one of the first bowlers to gain media attention for using the two handed approach style to deliver his shot. He has won 30 PBA titles, including a record 14 major championships which makes him the only one of eight bowlers in PBA tour history to achieve 30 wins.
