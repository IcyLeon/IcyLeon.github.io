2017 - Academic Disaster

During the mid-term examination, I failed all of my subjects except english and literature which is only a grade 5 and the rest were Ungraded. Overall, my EMB3 is 29 points which is 1 point away from retaining. It was a disaster. My teachers told me that I have a high chance of retaining if I do not take my academic seriously. However, during the june holidays, I had a wake up call. I went to a tuition center called MAVIS TUTORIAL CENTER at Kovan Heartland Mall for Mathematics and Science. In the end, I managed to get a EMB3 of 19 points.

2018 - Right Decision, Wrong Move

B Division is one of the most toughest competition for me because standards are higher than C Division. Before entering the competition, my coaches were conducting a selection process to see who gets to bowl in the competition. I was selected as I bowled above average of a score of 150+ during that time. However, it took a turn for the worst. During training (before competition), I did not bowl very well and was scoring 100+ which is below average. I felt that I would cause our ranking to drop so I decided to give my spot to one of my CCA mates. Although it is the worst decision I ever made, but I had no regrets since I "saved" our ranking.

2019 - Domination

One of my most exciting and memorable moments is when I got full marks for Science(Chemistry) for the first time. I remember when I got back from school after the B Division Bowling Competition at Orchid Country Club, my classmates were cheering and congratulate me which gave me confusion. During Exams, I became top in class for Mathematics and Science(Chemistry) and got a EMB3 of 12 points which I am eligible for the polytechnic foundation programme (PFP). For the first time ever, I dominate the top in class for Science which brought me feelings of exuberance and I cannot forget that.
